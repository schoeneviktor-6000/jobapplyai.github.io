What the ATS match score measures

We compare the job description with your CV and check if the most important requirements show up clearly in an ATS-friendly format.

  • Keyword coverage: skills, tools, and responsibilities found in your CV.
  • Priority terms: we weight the must-haves higher than nice-to-haves.
  • ATS basics: clear headings, readable structure, and consistent wording.
  • Keyword gaps: you see missing terms we recommend adding, only if they match your experience.

FAQ

Questions about CV tailoring.

If you have a specific setup such as industry, location, or seniority, reach out and we’ll confirm what’s possible.

Can I tailor from any website?
Yes. You can paste any job description manually, and the Chrome extension is designed to import the active job page directly into CV Studio.
Does CV Studio invent experience or fake details?
No. Tailoring should improve clarity and relevance, not fabricate experience. If something is missing, we recommend changes only when it matches your real background.
Can I edit the tailored CV before exporting?
Yes. CV Studio keeps you in control. You can adjust bullet points, experience, skills, and profile before you export the final PDF.
What does the ATS match score mean?
It’s a practical indicator of how well your CV’s wording aligns with a specific job description, including keywords, priority terms, and formatting basics. It is not a guarantee of an interview.
Do I need to rewrite my whole CV each time?
No. The product reuses your stored base CV, then tailors it per role so you do not have to start from scratch for every application.
