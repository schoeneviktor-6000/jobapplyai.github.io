ATS score

A match score you can actually use.

Every tailored CV comes with an ATS match score and a keyword gap list — so you can see what improved and why.

What the ATS match score measures

We compare the job description with your CV and check if the most important requirements show up clearly in an ATS-friendly format.

  • Keyword coverage: skills, tools, and responsibilities found in your CV.
  • Priority terms: we weight the “must-haves” higher than nice-to-haves.
  • ATS basics: clear headings, readable structure, and consistent wording.
  • Keyword gaps: you see missing terms we recommend adding (only if they match your experience).

FAQ

Answers to the most common questions.

If you have a specific setup (industry, location, seniority), reach out — we’ll confirm what’s possible.

Do you really apply from my own email?
Yes. After you connect your Google account, applications can be sent from your Gmail so outreach looks natural and credible.
Do you invent experience or “fake” details?
No. Tailoring should improve clarity and relevance, not fabricate experience. If something is missing, we recommend changes only when it matches your real background.
Can I change targets or pause anytime?
Yes. You can update your role/location preferences as your search evolves and pause if you want to stop outreach for a period.
What does the ATS match score mean?
It’s a practical indicator of how well your CV’s wording aligns with a specific job description (keywords, priority terms, and formatting basics). It’s not a guarantee of an interview.
Is this “mass spam”?
No. The goal is consistent volume with relevance. We prioritize matching and role-specific narrative so your outreach protects your reputation.
