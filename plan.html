Questions people usually ask

What counts toward the quota?
Each successful tailored CV generation counts once, whether it starts from the extension or from pasted job text.
Do I need Auto-apply to use CV Studio?
No. CV Studio is the main product. Auto-apply is optional and handled separately for users who want more than tailoring.
What if self-serve billing is still being finalized?
You still get a guided fallback, so you can signal buying intent and get help instead of hitting a dead end.
