Privacy Policy

How jobmejob handles data

This Privacy Policy explains how jobmejob.com and the CV Studio Tailor Chrome extension collect, use, and protect information when you use our website, Chrome extension, and related services.

Effective date: March 30, 2026 Applies to website + Chrome extension Contact: team@jobmejob.com

Who we are

jobmejob provides job application tooling, CV tailoring, and related workflow features through jobmejob.com. This policy also covers the CV Studio Tailor Chrome extension, which helps users scrape a job posting and open it in jobmejob CV Studio.

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact us at team@jobmejob.com.

What we collect

Depending on how you use jobmejob, we may collect and process the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, and authentication details provided through sign-in providers.
  • Profile and job search information, such as target roles, preferred locations, work preferences, and other settings you enter.
  • CV and application materials, including uploaded CV files, extracted CV text, tailored CV output, and related edits.
  • Job-related information, including job titles, companies, job descriptions, links, and keywords used to tailor your CV or support application workflows.
  • Usage and technical information, such as browser type, approximate region, timestamps, logs, and diagnostic information needed to operate and protect the service.
  • Support communications if you contact us.

How we use data

We use personal data to operate and improve jobmejob, including to:

  • authenticate users and secure accounts;
  • store and process uploaded CVs and profile data;
  • generate tailored CVs, keyword analysis, and related AI-assisted output;
  • help users review jobs, job descriptions, and application materials;
  • provide support, troubleshoot issues, and monitor service reliability;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.
We do not sell personal data. We do not use or transfer user data for purposes unrelated to the service’s core job-application and CV-tailoring functionality.

Chrome extension

The CV Studio Tailor Chrome extension is designed for one narrow purpose: helping a user scrape the currently open job posting and send it into jobmejob CV Studio.

When you use the extension, it may access:

  • the URL of the active job posting page;
  • visible page content needed to identify the job title, company, and description;
  • the current tab state so the side panel can refresh when the job page changes.

The extension does not monitor browsing activity in the background, does not sell browsing data, and does not inject advertising. It reads job-posting content only when the user opens the extension or explicitly requests a scrape.

After scraping, the extension opens jobmejob.com and passes the extracted job data into the website so the user can continue the CV Studio flow.

How data is shared

We may share data with service providers that help us operate the service, for example providers that support hosting, storage, authentication, analytics, email delivery, or AI-assisted generation. These providers may process data on our behalf under contractual or technical controls.

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law, to respond to valid legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of jobmejob, our users, or others.

Data retention

We keep data for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain account history, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and how the feature is used.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data. No online service can guarantee absolute security, but we work to reduce risk and limit access to data where appropriate.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to certain processing of your personal data. To make a request, contact team@jobmejob.com.

International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than the one where you live. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page and may provide additional notice where appropriate.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at team@jobmejob.com.
